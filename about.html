So what is Studio Winston?

Hello!

Welcome to website StudioWinston.com, a hub for our creative endeavors made possible by the help of some great people. As of now, we are in the process of completing our interactive story Smif Adventures. A future demo will give a small glimpse of it's story where the reader can chose different actions and actually rewrite certain parts of the story for a more personalized narrative. We look forward to producing more content and strive to keep our content family friendly.

The story goes that after your bonehead brother goes missing in a series of bizarre events, only you can bring him back! With your own wits, you must make critical decisions in order to make sure not only your brother makes it back home for dinner, but you as well. In addition to making decisions as the main character, you can simply rewrite certain parts of the story in a mad lib fashion in order to personalize your adventure to your own needs. Can you make it through without failing? How will your story differ from other readers? Only way to find out!
